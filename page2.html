The Sweet on Sydney Menu

Did you know that Sydney Sweeney--the famous actress--is launching a new menu! Starring in the new menu, is Sydney’s Signature Scoop, which features Rainbow Sherbet in a chocolate-dipped waffle cone with rainbow sprinkles and topped with gummi bear minis. Along with the new scoop is a new, refreshing beverage, Sydney’s Signature Fizz, which blends Rainbow Sherbet with STARRY™ lemon lime soda* and once again has a gummi bear minis topping. For an additional charge, this drink will be served in an exclusive color-changing collectable gummi bear cup.
